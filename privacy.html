Privacy Policy

Last updated: November 16, 2025

Introduction

DelayDone ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information when you use our mobile application.

Information Collection and Use

DelayDone is designed with privacy in mind. We want to be clear about our data practices:

Data Storage

  • Local Storage Only: All your tasks, delays, and preferences are stored locally on your device.
  • No Cloud Sync: We do not collect, transmit, or store any of your personal data on our servers.
  • Complete Privacy: Your task information never leaves your device.

Information We Do NOT Collect

  • Personal identification information (name, email, phone number)
  • Task content or data
  • Location data
  • Device identifiers
  • Usage analytics
  • Crash reports

Permissions

DelayDone may request the following permissions:

Notifications

  • Purpose: To send you reminders for your delayed tasks
  • Control: You can enable or disable notifications in your device settings

Third-Party Services

DelayDone does not use any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

In-App Purchases

If you make in-app purchases, the transaction is processed by Apple's App Store. We do not have access to your payment information. Please refer to Apple's Privacy Policy for information about how they handle your data.

Children's Privacy

Our app does not collect any personal information from anyone, including children under the age of 13.

Data Security

Since all data is stored locally on your device, the security of your information depends on your device's security measures. We recommend:

  • Using a strong device passcode
  • Keeping your device's operating system up to date
  • Being cautious about device access

Data Deletion

You can delete all your data at any time by:

  • Deleting individual tasks within the app
  • Uninstalling the DelayDone app from your device

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at:

Email: support@delaydone.app
